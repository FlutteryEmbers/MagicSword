Magic Sword

The magic sword is a “smart weapon” that hosts a AR MMORPG, based on the motion sensing system and battle server online.

This magic weapon allows players to duel with distant enemies and to combat with them using customized body motion, recognized as attack or defense. All the attack motion can be cataloged into two modes. One is great attack that can deal massive damage to enemies while the opponents can use defensive motion to shield the damage. Another one is light attack dealing little damage that cannot be shielded, but enemies’ great attack will cause more damage in a unit of time. Defeating enemies requires players to choose correct timing release skills.
